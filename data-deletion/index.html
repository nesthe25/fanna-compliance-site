Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Users can request deletion of their data by emailing support@mantradigi.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@mantradigi.com.
  2. Include your name, connected account, and request details.
  3. We will process deletion within a reasonable time after verifying the request.
  4. Users may also remove app access from their Meta account settings.

Meta App Access

If you connected a Facebook or Instagram account through Meta, you can remove FANNA's access from your Meta account settings. Removing access may stop future data collection from that connected account, but you may still email us to request deletion of stored data associated with your account.

Contact

For deletion requests or questions, contact support@mantradigi.com.
